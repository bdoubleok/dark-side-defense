## 🎨 NEW COVERAGES (PHASE 4):

### **Quarter-Quarter-Half**
**Concept:** Two defenders play quarters, one plays deep half
**Structure:** Hybrid split-field coverage
**When:** vs elite QB, advanced pattern-match situations
**Only in:** Passing Downs (Obvious) at 8%

**How it Works:**
- Boundary side: Two defenders quarter the field
- Field side: One safety takes deep half
- Adapts to route distribution
- Advanced NFL concept

---

### **Fire Zone 2**
**Concept:** Five-man rush, different drop pattern than Fire Zone 3
**Structure:** End drops, LB rushes opposite
**Coverage Behind:** 3-under, 3-deep zone
**When:** 3rd & 12+, obvious pass
**Blitz Rate:** 10% on obvious pass (12% total blitz)

**How it Works:**
- Show 6 rushers pre-snap
- Bring 5, one DL drops
- Creates confusion for OL
- Different from Sim Pressure 3 (different personnel drops)
