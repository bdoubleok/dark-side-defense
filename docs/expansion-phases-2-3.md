## 💡 NEW COVERAGE CONCEPTS EXPLAINED:

### **Cover 2 Cloud**
**Concept:** Corners force run (like linebackers), safeties play deep halves
**Pre-Snap:** Looks like Cover 3 (disguise)
**Post-Snap:** Corners come down hard on run, safeties protect deep
**When:** vs outside zone, stretch, toss plays
**Benefit:** Disguise + run support

### **Cover 3 Buzz**
**Concept:** Safety buzzes to flat, coverage rotates to Cover 3
**Pre-Snap:** Looks like Cover 2 (two deep safeties)
**Post-Snap:** Strong safety buzzes down to flat, weak safety rotates middle, becomes Cover 3
**When:** Confuse QB reads, disrupt route timing
**Benefit:** Disguise + QB confusion

### **Cover 1 Robber**
**Concept:** Man coverage with free safety robbing middle
**Pre-Snap:** Similar to regular Cover 1
**Post-Snap:** Man coverage on all receivers, safety lurks in middle to rob crossing routes
**When:** Change-up after 3-4 zone plays, vs crossing routes
**Benefit:** Stops crossers, change-up

### **Cover 4 Solo**
**Concept:** Weak side plays quarters, strong side plays man
**Pre-Snap:** Looks like regular quarters
**Post-Snap:** Split-field - weak quarters, strong man
**When:** vs unbalanced formations, star WR to one side
**Benefit:** Bracket star receiver with man, protect weak side with zone
